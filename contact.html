Frequently Asked Questions

Is Smart Health Navigator a substitute for medical advice?

No. While our tools provide useful information, they are not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider for medical concerns.

How accurate is the Symptom Checker?

Our Symptom Checker provides suggestions based on the information you provide, but it cannot make a definitive diagnosis. It's designed to give you general guidance and help you decide when to seek professional care.

Is my personal health information secure?

Yes. We take data privacy very seriously. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information, and we never share your personal data without your explicit consent.

How do I provide feedback or report an issue?

You can use the contact form on this page to send feedback or report issues. We appreciate your input and strive to continuously improve our tools.
